Giving Back and Going Green

This year for our holiday gifts, we decided to focus on giving back to society. Bombas socks, which many people received, donates one pair of socks per pair of socks purchased, so together we've donated 35 pairs of socks. In addition to the socks, we decided to donate funds to charity on behalf of our friends and family and include that as part of their gift. For each present, we donated $100 to a few climate change non-profits that you can see below.

Coalition for Rainforest Nations. Prevent deforestation, support reforestation.

https://www.rainforestcoalition.org/

Clean Air Task Force. Lobbying for better policies.

https://www.catf.us/

Climate Emergency Fund. Grants for climate activism.

https://climateemergencyfund.org/
